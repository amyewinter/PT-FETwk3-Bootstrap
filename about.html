Why do you care so much about poo?

Poo is gross. No one wants poo on their shoe.
Poo is smelly. No one wants to smell poo.
Poo is germy. Harmful bugs and parasites in dog poop left lying around can make dogs sick. For example, they might lick poo-contaminated dirt or water from their paws after frolicking at the dog park.
Dog poo can make people sick. Many of the harmful organisms in dog poop can be passed to humans through water contamination and make us sick.
Poo wrecks the landscape. The city would be buried in dog poop before the amount all our dogs produce has time to break down naturally. Meanwhile, leaving it around makes other dogs want to go in the same spot, which burns the grass and leaves bare spots.
